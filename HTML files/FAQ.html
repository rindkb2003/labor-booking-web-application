Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Labor Booking System?

The Labor Booking System is an online platform designed to facilitate the hiring process for skilled laborers in various trades within District Sanghar.

How do I sign up as a worker?

To sign up as a worker, navigate to the Sign Up page, fill out the required information, and submit the form. You will need to provide details such as your name, contact information, skills, and upload necessary documents.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact customer support by visiting the Contact Us page and filling out the form with your query or by emailing us directly at rindbaba2003@gmail.com.

What types of workers can I hire through the platform?

Our platform allows you to hire various types of workers including carpenters, electricians, painters, masons, and plumbers.

Is there a fee to use the Labor Booking System?

There are no registration fees for workers as well as customers.
